Easy To Socialize

Our community is one of the best place to socialize. Full of people with different background, hobbies, and other stuff that made it interesting to talk to.

Our community is always active 24/7, with responsive admin/moderator. Admin and moderator are ready to help you out with everything that the member needed.

One Of The Largest Out There...

One of the largest Indonesian gaming/discord community out there, that is Hellbound. With thousands daily active member, and responsive admin/moderator. It is one of the best community to get into right now.

Wide Variety Of People

With wide variety of member, finding a topic or someone that matches your criteria is not abig deal. From your easy to go gaming friends to a cinephile, there are tons of people that matches any type of criteria.
